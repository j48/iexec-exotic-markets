Exotic Prediction Markets

Oracle Factory Edition

Powered By

This project allows for the creation of decentralized exotic prediction markets where users can bet on whether a certain on-chain oracle value will be over or under a predefined value after the completion time. This project leverages a smart contract to handle predictions and the iExec Oracle Factory for oracle results.

The ERC-1155 smart contract acts as the market creator and escrow to hold collateral for each market. When a user places a bet they are issued tokens tied to that specific market and bet. After a market closes and the oracle value is verified, the user can swap their winning tokens for the appropriate percentage of the total market pool.
